Hair Tips

    PROTECT HAIR AT NIGHT

  • Be sure to tie your hair up into 1-2 braids at night to ensure freshness of hair

    • MOISTURIZE REGULARLY

  • Be sure to moisturize your bundles using our hair oil! Use every two days for the full effect.

    • AVOID TOO MUCH HEAT

  • Use heat only up to 400 degrees. Too much heat will cause the hair to break.
